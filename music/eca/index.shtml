Song Details

This song plays during the cutscene from the attraction mode of the 1999 Sega arcade game Emergency Call Ambulance.

Information for this game is rather sparse, but it surviving arcade cabinets and boards exist, and it may be played on the appropriate emulator. Gameplay can also be found on YouTube.
