Song Details

The opening for the grand route of Lose's 2016 visual novel Maitetsu, played in the main menu once you unlock the grand route's backdrop.

You can purchase a digital copy of the game on Steam, or visit their website for information on purchasing it for Nintendo Switch or PlayStation.
