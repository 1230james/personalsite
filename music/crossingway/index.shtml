Song Details

The song played during the grand opening for Sprite's visual novel Aokana: Four Rhythms Across the Blue.

You can purchase a digital copy of the game on NekoNyanSoft's website. A few portions of the game contain adult content, so be advised. An all-ages version is available on Steam, too, at the time of writing.
