Information

Song Details

The theme from the opening trailer for Lose's upcoming 2020 visual novel Maitetsu - Last Run!!. Not much is known regarding the visual novel in English, but those who can read Japanese can visit their website for more information. An English localization has not been confirmed, and probably will not be for some time.
