Song Details

The opening theme song for Kyoto Animation's 2007 anime adaptation of Kagami Yoshimizu's comic strip Lucky Star.

  • Name: もってけ！セーラーふく (Motteke! Sailor Fuku)
  • Source: Lucky Star
  • Composer: 神前 暁 (Satoru Kōsaki)
  • Released: 2007
