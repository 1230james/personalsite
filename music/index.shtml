Sheet Music

Welcome to James's Sheet Music!

This page is where you can find all my music transcriptions, new and old. I use MuseScore to create all my scores, and then I upload them here for the public to enjoy. I always strive to make my transcriptions sound as true to the original song as possible, although I am by no means a professional and I also edit sections that sound awkward when transcribed, so 100% accuracy is not guaranteed.

Featured Work

Art of Blade

The background music played in the Contingency Contract #2 lobby in Hypergryph's 2019 mobile tower defense gacha game Arknights.

Most Recent

Rule The World

One of the background tracks and the main menu track for Paradox Interactive's society-building economic strategy game Victoria 3.

No profits are made from any of my works directly, nor do I ever intend to. Music transcription is purely a passion project/hobby I enjoy doing with songs I love listening to alongside my other hobbies. All my works are available to the public free of charge.

If you'd like to show your support, please (1) check out the sources for the songs I transcribe to help out the original creators, and (2) share this page and/or my works with other people. It makes me happy knowing that there are people out there who are listening to what I've done. If that isn't enough for you, feel free to stop by my Ko-fi page. Anything you can do to support artists and myself is greatly appreciated!
