IndEC

Sid Meier's Civilization III

Information

Song Details

One of the background music tracks from Civ3 that plays while your civilization is in the Industrial Era. When playing as a European civilization, this is also the first song that plays upon entering the Industrial Era.

You can purchase a copy of Civ3 on Steam.

  • Name: IndEC (as referenced in the game files)
  • Source: Sid Meier's Civilization III
  • Composer: Roger Briggs
  • Released: 2001
