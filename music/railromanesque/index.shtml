Information

Song Details

The main theme for Lose's 2016 visual novel Maitetsu, played during the opening for Hachiroku's route and the trailer.

You can purchase a digital copy of the game on Steam, or visit their website for information on purchasing it for Nintendo Switch or PlayStation.

  • Name: レイル・ロマネスク (Rail Romanesque)
  • Source: Maitetsu
  • Composer: 佐久間 きらら (Sakuma Kirara)
  • Released: 2015
