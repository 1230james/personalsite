Song Details

The background music played in the Contingency Contract #2 lobby in Hypergryph's 2019 mobile tower defense gacha game Arknights. This song is formally titled "Operation Blade".

Arknights is free to play, and can be found on the iOS App Store and Google Play Store.

  • Name: Operation Blade / Art of Blade
  • Source: Arknights
  • Composer: Obadiah Brown-Beach
  • Released: 2020
