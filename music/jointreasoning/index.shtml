Song Details

One of the three types of the song Joint Reasoning: The Great Deduction from Capcom's 2017 game Dai Gyakuten Saiban: Naruhodō Ryūnosuke no Bōken (大逆転裁判 -成歩堂龍ノ介の冒險-), called The Great Ace Attorney in the English fan-translation. Three versions of this song play throughout the game, with Type A being the most common, during segments where the protagonist and Sherlock Holmes are performing what is referred to as a "great deduction" in investigation portions of the game.

The English fan-translation can be found on Scarlet Study's website, the website of the team responsible for the translation. Note that a legitimate copy of the original game is required; this website is not an outlet for piracy.
