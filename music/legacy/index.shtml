Legacy Works

Overview

This page consists of some older works I did years ago. While I do not want to permanently hide them, I personally think they are not very representative of my abilities as a transposer, so I do not wish to showcase them to the same degree as my other, higher-quality works. Downloads and links to playback videos are provided where available.

All works on this page are published under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0.

Songs

Intruder Alert

Team Fortress 2

Intruder Alert from Team Fortress 2. It plays as one of the main menu songs upon booting the game, and a portion of it also plays during Valve's Meet the Spy short.
