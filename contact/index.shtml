Contact Me

Listed below are various methods to get into touch with me.

Methods are listed in order of preference from top to bottom, with the most preferred method at the top.

Email

Click here to send a message.

Email is being sent to a "buffer" address to protect my primary email. If I feel it is appropriate, then I will move correspondence to my primary email, so do not be surprised if the reply you get is from a different address. I will likely (although it is not guaranteed) send an email from the buffer address to inform you that the conversation is moving to my primary address with my primary address listed for you to verify.

Please note that email responses are not guaranteed; that is, I may read your email, but a reply is not guaranteed.

If you already have my primary email address, you are free to contact me through that address.

Telephone

If, somehow, you have my telephone number (through my résumé, perhaps), then please refrain from attempting to contact me via voice unless it is absolutely urgent. I reject virtually every call.

If you absolutely must contact me via phone, please keep calling multiple times in a row. If I notice that the same number is persistent on calling me, it will be proof to me that the 500 millionth robo-caller isn't the one trying to call me.
